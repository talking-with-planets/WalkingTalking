Who we are?

Walking Talking is vibrant community for immigrants in Japan, dedicated to mutual aid among professionals and students from diverse backgrounds. Focused on cultural exchange, support, and growth, we create a space for celebrating multicultural unity and enriching experiences in Japan. Join us in fostering a supportive, interconnected community.
