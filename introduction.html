Introduction

Welcome to Walking Talking, a community dedicated to promoting health and social interaction through walking and conversation. Our group brings together people from diverse backgrounds to explore different locales while engaging in bilingual conversation in Japanese and English. Whether you're looking to improve your physical fitness, practice language skills, or simply make new friends, WalkingTalking offers a unique and enriching experience.
